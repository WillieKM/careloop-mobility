Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellations

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations when possible.

  • More than 24 hours notice: No charge.
  • Same-day cancellations: May incur a late cancellation fee.
  • No-shows: Full fare or a no-show fee may apply.

2. Refunds

Refunds are issued when:

  • A prepaid booking is cancelled more than 24 hours in advance.
  • We are unable to provide service due to operational issues.

Refunds are typically processed within 3–7 business days.

3. Driver Arrival & Wait Time

  • Drivers wait up to 10 minutes at pickup unless prearranged.
  • Extended wait time may incur additional charges.

4. Weather, Traffic & Delays

Severe weather, traffic, or road closures may impact travel times. Refunds are not issued for delays caused by conditions outside our control.
