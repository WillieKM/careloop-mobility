FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I schedule?

24–48 hours is recommended. Same-day availability varies.

Do you support wheelchair riders?

Yes. Ambulatory and wheelchair rides are available (no stretchers).

Do you work with clinics?

Yes. We coordinate recurring treatments, discharge rides, and schedules with clinics and care teams.

Can a caregiver ride along?

Yes. One caregiver may ride at no additional cost when seating allows.

Do you schedule return trips?

Yes. We schedule return trips based on appointment length or facility discharge time.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please notify us as early as possible. Ideally contact should be made 24 hours in advance.
