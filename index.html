Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I schedule?

24–48 hours is recommended. Same-day trips may be available based on schedule.

Do you support wheelchair riders?

Yes. We provide ambulatory and wheelchair transportation (no stretcher services).

Can a caregiver ride along?

Yes. One caregiver or family member can ride at no additional cost when space allows.

Do you schedule return trips?

We can schedule return trips based on appointment length or facility discharge time.
